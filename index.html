Guiding patients from treatment to recovery

It's natural for people to be inquisitive about their condition, their treatment and the prognosis. So patients can access audio and video tutorials about any surgeries, plus reading materials which explain things in a simple way. This educates patients without taking up your medical team's time.And when patients are more at ease about their care plan, they're more likely to give you a 5-star rating.

Patient expectations perfectly matched

Patients are more relaxed when they know what's going on. Their health and care dashboard gives an at-a-glance view of their daily activities, medical appointments, upcoming tests, vital statistics and any exercises they need to perform. You can also empower them to play a part in their recovery, with further care options upon discharge.

Age and culture appropriate interfaces for each patient

You can personalise the layout and content of the in-room iPad for different patients based on their profile. From child-friendly design options and simpler version for senior patients to bigger font sizing and voice activated controls for the visually impaired. You can also make prayer and meditation materials available based on patients' faith.

Audio and video calls to keep patients connected
